Services

Prop is your Finance Team, in your business, under your control. We take care of the boring tedious but crucial parts of running a business! Leaving you to focus on what you love. We make sure your accounts, human resources, admin and tech support are running at their best. So that you can focus on the important things like making money.

Finance

Prop is your finance team!! We prepare your accounting paperwork, invoices, payments and reporting for your approval.

Tech Support
